Flapjacks

Overview

An easy-to-follow recipe (suitable for beginners), that will leave you with a batch of the most mouth-watering and simply delicious flapjacks!

Ingredients

  • 125g brown sugar
  • 250g oats
  • 125g butter
  • 1tsp salt
  • 50g maple syrup

Method

  1. Melt the butter.
  2. Mix the brown sugar, salt, maple syrup, and melted butter together.
  3. Add the oats to the mixture and mix to ensure the oats are coated.
  4. Grease a baking tray, and pour the mixture into the tray.
  5. Turn the oven to gas mark 6 / 200Â°C. Put the baking tray in the oven for 20 minutes.

And you're done!
