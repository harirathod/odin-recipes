Panini

Overview

Not only are these paninis easy to make, but they're also delightfully yummy! What's not to love?

Ingredients

  • Slices of panini bread
  • Grated cheddar cheese
  • Butter (or margarine)
  • 1tsp salt
  • 50g maple syrup

Method

  2. Mix the brown sugar, salt, maple syrup, and melted butter together.
